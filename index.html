About

Meet Tamar

My passion is working with individuals to maximize their health and well-being. Whether as a bedside nurse in the intensive care unit, a nurse practitioner in a specialty clinic, or a life care planner determining long-term healthcare needs, I have demonstrated a commitment to compassionate quality care.


I am proud to announce the launch of Summit Life Care Planning, LLC, which provides life care planning services for individuals with complex medical needs. I bring nearly a decade of experience in the field of Life Care Planning and am passionate about providing comprehensive, quality reports with integrity, efficiency and excellence. With a strong clinical background in pediatrics, I have special expertise in addressing the long-term needs of children with cerebral palsy, autism and a variety of rare genetic conditions. I also have extensive testimony experience, having testified on more than 50 occasions and having been admitted as an expert witness in more than half a dozen states.
